# 📖 Chapter 17: Retrieval-Augmented Transformers (REALM)

---

## 🔷 1. The Core Problem

Even powerful Transformer models have a limitation:

👉 They rely only on **what they learned during training**

---

### ❌ Problems

* Knowledge can become **outdated**
* Cannot access **new or rare information**
* May give **incorrect answers**

---

### 🧠 Example

Question:
👉 *“What are the latest changes in a law?”*

👉 A normal model may not know recent updates

---

## 🔷 2. What is Retrieval-Augmented AI?

---

### 💡 Definition

Retrieval-Augmented models:

👉 **combine a Transformer with an external knowledge source**

---

### 🧠 One-Line Idea

👉
“Don’t rely only on memory — look up information when needed.”

---

## 🔷 3. Real Model Example

👉 REALM

---

### 💡 What REALM Does

* Searches a knowledge base
* Retrieves relevant documents
* Uses them to answer questions

---

## 🔷 4. How REALM Works (Simple Flow)

---

### Step 1: Input Query

👉 *“What causes diabetes?”*

---

### Step 2: Retrieval

👉 Model searches external data:

* Articles
* Documents
* Databases

---

### Step 3: Select Relevant Information

👉 Picks the most useful content

---

### Step 4: Transformer Processing

👉 Uses retrieved data to understand

---

### Step 5: Generate Answer

👉 Produces accurate response
