# 📖 Chapter 14: Vision Transformers (ViT)

---

## 🔷 1. The Big Idea

Transformers were first built for **text**.
But researchers asked:

👉 *Can we use the same idea for images?*

👉 That led to **Vision Transformers (ViT)**

---

### 🧠 One-Line Idea

👉
“A Vision Transformer treats an image like a sequence of small pieces and applies attention to understand it.”

---

## 🔷 2. Real Model Example

👉 Vision Transformer

---

## 🔷 3. The Problem with Images

---

Images are:

* 2D (height × width)
* Made of pixels

👉 Transformers expect:

* 1D sequence (like words)

---

### ❓ So what is the solution?

---

## 🔷 4. Solution: Convert Image into Patches

---

### 💡 Key Idea

👉 Break image into **small squares (patches)**

---

## 🔷 5. Step-by-Step Working of ViT

---

### Step 1: Input Image

👉 Example: picture of a cat

---

### Step 2: Divide into Patches

* Image → small squares

---

### Step 3: Flatten Each Patch

* Each patch → converted into numbers

---

### Step 4: Add Positional Encoding

* Model knows where each patch is

---

### Step 5: Apply Transformer Encoder

* Self-attention connects patches
* Learns relationships

---

### Step 6: Output

👉 Model predicts:

* “cat”
* “dog”
* or other labels

---

## 🔷 6. Why Attention Works for Images

---

### 🧠 Example

In an image of a dog:

* Ear ↔ head
* tail ↔ body

👉 Model connects distant parts using attention

---

### 🟡 Analogy

You look at:

* eyes
* ears
* shape

👉 Then identify object

---

## 🔷 7. ViT vs CNN (Important Comparison)

---

| Feature     | CNN           | Vision Transformer |
| ----------- | ------------- | ------------------ |
| Approach    | Local filters | Global attention   |
| Focus       | Nearby pixels | Entire image       |
| Data need   | Less          | More               |
| Flexibility | Limited       | High               |

---

## 🔷 8. Advantages of Vision Transformers

---

### ✅ Captures global relationships

### ✅ Flexible architecture

### ✅ Works well with large data

### ✅ Can outperform CNNs

---

## 🔷 9. Limitations

---

### ❌ Needs large dataset

### ❌ High computation

### ❌ Training is expensive

---

## 🔷 10. Where Vision Transformers Are Used

---

### 🖼️ Image classification

### 🧬 Medical imaging (X-rays, MRI)

### 🚗 Self-driving cars

### 🛰️ Satellite image analysis
