# 📖 Chapter 25: Transformers in Computer Vision

---

## 🔷 1. What is Computer Vision?

**Computer Vision** means:

👉 Teaching computers to **see and understand images**

---

### 🧠 One-Line Idea

👉
“Computer Vision helps machines understand what is inside an image.”

---

## 🔷 2. From CNN to Transformers

Earlier, image tasks used:

* CNN (Convolutional Neural Networks)

Now, we also use Transformers like:

👉 Vision Transformer

---

### 💡 Key Difference

* CNN → looks at small regions
* Transformer → looks at entire image using attention

---

## 🔷 3. How Transformers Work on Images

---

### Step-by-Step Idea

1. Image is divided into patches
2. Each patch becomes a vector
3. Positional encoding is added
4. Transformer processes relationships
5. Output is predicted

---

## 🔷 4. What Transformers Learn in Images

---

### 🧠 Example

Image of a dog:

* Head ↔ body
* legs ↔ movement

👉 Model connects distant parts

---

### 🟡 Analogy

You identify a person by:

* face
* body
* posture

👉 Not just one small part

## 🔷 5. Key Tasks in Computer Vision

---

### 🟢 1. Image Classification

👉 Identify object in image

Example:

* Dog / Cat / Car

---

### 🟢 2. Object Detection

👉 Find and locate objects

Example:

* Detect multiple people in image

---

### 🟢 3. Image Segmentation

👉 Divide image into parts

Example:

* Separate tumor from healthy tissue

---

### 🟢 4. Image Captioning

👉 Describe image in text

Example:
👉 “A dog is running in the park”

## 🔷 7. Advantages of Transformers in Vision

---

### ✅ Capture global relationships

### ✅ Better understanding of complex scenes

### ✅ Flexible architecture

### ✅ Works well with large data

---

## 🔷 8. Limitations

---

### ❌ Needs large dataset

### ❌ High computation cost

### ❌ Training is expensive

## 🔷 9. CNN vs Transformer (Quick Comparison)

---

| Feature          | CNN     | Transformer |
| ---------------- | ------- | ----------- |
| Focus            | Local   | Global      |
| Data requirement | Lower   | Higher      |
| Flexibility      | Limited | High        |
