# 📖 Chapter 23: Transformers in Healthcare

---

## 🔷 1. Why Healthcare Needs AI

Healthcare deals with:

* Huge amounts of data
* Complex patient histories
* Time-sensitive decisions

👉 Doctors often need help to:

* analyze reports
* detect patterns
* make faster decisions

---

### 🧠 One-Line Idea

👉
“Transformers help doctors understand large medical data quickly and accurately.”

---

## 🔷 2. What Transformers Bring to Healthcare

Transformers can:

👉 Read + understand + generate medical information

---

### 💡 Key Capabilities

* Understand clinical text
* Analyze medical images
* Predict diseases
* Assist in diagnosis

## 🔷 3. Medical Text Analysis

---

### 💡 Problem

Doctors write long reports:

* discharge summaries
* prescriptions
* case histories

---

### ✅ Transformer Solution

Using models like BERT:

👉 Extract useful information automatically

---

### 🧠 Example

Input:
👉 “Patient has fever, cough, and fatigue”

Output:
👉 Possible infection detected
