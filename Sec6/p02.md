# 📖 Chapter 24: Transformers in Education

---

## 🔷 1. Why Education Needs AI

Education today faces challenges:

* Large number of students
* Different learning speeds
* Limited personal attention

👉 Teachers cannot give **individual support to everyone**

---

### 🧠 One-Line Idea

👉
“Transformers help provide personalized learning for every student.”

---

Transformers can:

👉 Understand questions
👉 Generate explanations
👉 Adapt to student needs

---

### 💡 Key Capabilities

* Answer student queries
* Create study material
* Evaluate answers
* Translate content
