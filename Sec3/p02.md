# 📖 Chapter 11: Decoder-Based Models

---

## 🔷 1. What are Decoder-Based Models?

**Decoder-based models** use only the **decoder part** of a Transformer.

👉 Their main job is:

👉 **to generate text step-by-step**

---

### 🧠 One-Line Idea

👉
“Decoder models write — they generate sentences word by word.”

---

## 🔷 2. Real Model Example

A well-known decoder-based model is:

👉 GPT

---

### 💡 What GPT Does

* Generates text
* Completes sentences
* Answers questions
* Writes code and stories

---

## 🔷 3. How Decoder Models Work

---

### Step-by-Step Flow

1. Take input text
2. Predict next word
3. Add that word
4. Repeat the process

---

### 🧠 Example

Input:
👉 *“The sun rises in the”*

Output generation:

* east → sky → morning → …

👉 Generated one word at a time

---

## 🔷 4. Key Feature: Autoregressive Generation

---

### 💡 Meaning

👉 The model uses **previous words to predict the next word**

---

### 🧠 Example

👉 *“I am going to the”*

* Model predicts → “market”
* Now input becomes → *“I am going to the market”*

Then continues…

---

### 🟡 Analogy

Writing a sentence:

* You write one word
* Then think about the next

---

## 🔷 5. Masked Self-Attention (Important Concept)

---

### 💡 What happens?

While predicting a word:

👉 Model can only see **past words**, not future ones

---

### 🧠 Example

Sentence being generated:

👉 *“The dog chased the cat”*

When predicting “chased”:

* Model sees: “The dog”
  ❌ Cannot see: “cat”

---

### 🟡 Analogy

While writing:

* You don’t know future words
* You build sentence gradually

---

## 🔷 6. Why Masking is Important

---

### ✅ Prevents cheating

### ✅ Maintains correct order

### ✅ Enables natural language generation

---

## 🔷 7. Output of Decoder Models

---

👉 Final output is:

👉 **generated text (not just numbers)**

---

### 🧠 Example

Input:
👉 *“Once upon a time”*

Output:
👉 *“…there was a king who lived in a castle.”*

---

## 🔷 10. Limitations

---

### ❌ No true understanding (pattern-based)

---

## 🔷 11. Decoder vs Encoder (Quick View)

---

| Feature   | Encoder        | Decoder       |
| --------- | -------------- | ------------- |
| Role      | Understand     | Generate      |
| Output    | Representation | Text          |
| Direction | Bidirectional  | Left to right |
| Example   | BERT           | GPT           |
