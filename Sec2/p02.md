# 📖 Chapter 7: Decoder Block (GPT)

---

## 🔷 1. What is the Decoder Block?

The **Decoder** is the part of a Transformer that:

👉 **generates output step-by-step (usually text)**

Unlike the encoder (which understands), the decoder **creates new content**.

---

### 🧠 One-Line Idea

👉
“Decoder = Writer (it produces sentences word by word)”

---

## 🔷 2. Real Model Example

A well-known decoder-based model is:

👉 GPT

---

### 💡 What GPT Does

* Generates text
* Continues sentences
* Answers questions
* Writes code and stories

---

## 🔷 3. Why Decoder is Needed

Understanding alone is not enough.

👉 We also need a system that can:

* Produce answers
* Translate text
* Generate new ideas

---

### 🧠 Example

Input:
👉 *“Once upon a time…”*

Decoder Output:
👉 *“…there was a king who lived in a forest.”*

---

## 🔷 4. Structure of Decoder Block

The decoder has **three main components**:

---

### 🔵 1. Masked Self-Attention

* Looks at previous words only
* Cannot see future words

👉 This ensures correct sequence generation

---

### 🔵 2. Encoder-Decoder Attention (optional)

* Used when encoder is present
* Helps connect input with output

👉 (Not used in pure GPT)

---

### 🔵 3. Feedforward Neural Network

* Refines output
* Adds deeper understanding

---

## 🔷 5. Step-by-Step Working

---

### Step 1: Start with input

👉 *“The cat”*

---

### Step 2: Predict next word

👉 Model predicts: *“sat”*

---

### Step 3: Add predicted word

👉 Now input becomes:
*“The cat sat”*

---

### Step 4: Predict next word again

👉 Model predicts: *“on”*

---

👉 This continues until sentence is complete

---

## 🔷 6. What is Masked Attention?

---

### 💡 Key Idea

While generating a word:

👉 The model can only see **past words**, not future ones

---

### 🧠 Example

Sentence being generated:

👉 *“The dog chased the cat”*

When predicting **“chased”**, model sees:

* “The dog”
  ❌ Cannot see “cat” yet

---

### 🟡 Analogy

Writing a sentence:

* You don’t know future words
* You build sentence step-by-step

---

## 🔷 7. Why Masking is Important

---

### ✅ Prevents cheating

### ✅ Maintains correct sequence

### ✅ Enables proper language generation

---

## 🔷 8. Multiple Decoder Layers

---

### 💡 Like encoder:

Decoder is also stacked:

👉 Multiple layers improve quality

---

### 🧠 Example

* Layer 1 → basic grammar
* Layer 2 → context
* Layer 3 → fluent sentence
